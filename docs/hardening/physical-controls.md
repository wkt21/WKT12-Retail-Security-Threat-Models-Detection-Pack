# Physical Controls for Retail Security

Physical controls prevent barcode swap fraud, counterfeit labels, and rogue device placement.

---

## 1. Label-Stock Control
Track:
- Label rolls
- Print logs
- Authorized printers

Counterfeit labels often come from stolen stock.

---

## 2. Tamper-Evident Labels
Use labels that:
- Tear when removed
- Leave residue
- Cannot be cleanly replaced

---

## 3. Staff Training
Train staff to identify:
- Counterfeit coupons
- Tampered labels
- Suspicious customer behavior

---

## 4. Randomized SCO Audits
Audit:
- Bagging area weight
- Label integrity
- Scan cadence

Random audits deter fraud.

---

## 5. Secure ESL Controllers
Store ESL controllers:
- In locked cabinets
- With access logs
- Away from customer areas

---

## 6. Scanner Storage & Access Control
Prevent:
- Rogue scanner placement
- Unauthorized scanner swaps
- Scanner theft
